## 1. Project Overview

**Business:** Hammock Property Inspections, LLC
**Website:** hammockpropertyinspections.com
**Location:** Florida's Space Coast

**Objective:** Build a clean, modern, conversion-focused website that:
- Establishes trust immediately
- Clearly explains services and pricing
- Makes it easy to request an inspection

**Target Audience:**
- Homebuyers and homeowners in Florida
- Often under time pressure
- Want clarity, speed, and professionalism

**Services (in this order):**

2. **4-Point Inspection** — Focused evaluation of HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and roofing systems.
